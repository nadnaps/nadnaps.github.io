I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Physics of Fluids and Twente Max Planck Center for Complex Fluid Dynamics group. I recently graduated with a PhD under the supervision of Prof. Roberto Verzicco and Prof. Detlef Lohse for my thesis titled ‘Bubbles and drops in turbulent Taylor-Couette flow: numerical modelling and simulation’.

The main focus of my thesis was to understand the dynamics of bubbles and drops in a wall-bounded turbulent flows. I am also working in the field of fluid-structure interaction and in the development of new techniques and models for understanding large scale multi-physics flows.
